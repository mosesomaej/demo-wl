About

Highly skilled and motivated DevOps Engineer with a proven 5-year history of excelling in the design, deployment, and maintenance of cloud infrastructure. With profound passion for automation and technology, my expertise spans AWS Cloud, Infrastructure as Code (IaC), Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD), and application containerization and orchestration.

Leveraging my background in process optimization within the energy sector, I've successfully completed a DevOps and Cloud Compute bootcamp, and some of my certifications include the AWS Certified Solution Architect, HashiCorp Certified Terraform Associate and Certified Kubernetes Administrator, underscoring my unwavering commitment to continuous professional development
